9 Best Romance K-dramas Of All Time: That’ll Make You Fall In Love!

Do you love romantic films?

If so, you should discover the captivating world of Korean dramas, where each love story stirs those "butterflies in your stomach". K-Drama Haven presents viewers with tender and heartwarming plots infused with passion and romance, leaving a lasting mark on your heart.

Business Proposal (2022)

A romantic comedy about an office employee who, instead of a friend, goes on a blind date with the CEO of her company, hiding her identity. Then this ridiculous story develops into a beautiful love story.

Descendants of the Sun (2016)

A heartwarming romance about a dedicated soldier and a compassionate doctor entangled in a love story that unfolds amidst the chaos of their demanding professions.

Lovely Runner (2024)

Dorama follows Lim Sol as she repeatedly travels back in time to prevent her favorite idol, Ryu Seon-Jae, from taking his own life, only to uncover a deeper connection between them.

Crash Landing on You (2019-2020)

A popular drama about a wealthy South Korean heiress who accidentally lands in North Korea and falls in love with a North Korean army officer while navigating the tension between their countries.

Moon Lovers: Scarlet Heart Ryeo (2016)

Dorama follows a 21st-century woman who is transported back to the Goryeo Dynasty, where she becomes entangled in the lives and power and love struggles of several princes.

It's Okay to Not Be Okay (2020)

A man who denies love and a woman who knows nothing about love defy fate, fall in love and get to know themselves in the process of developing a relationship.

Alchemy of Souls (2022)

The cursed son of young wizards and a dead sorceress, whose spirit has temporarily settled in the body of another girl, must help each other and fight against powerful people who want to control people by moving souls.

Love Next Door (2024)

The story is about a girl who returns to Korea after a long life in the United States and tries to reboot her life full of mistakes. Upon returning, she meets a childhood friend — the ideal and successful son of her mother's friend.

Tale of the Nine Tailed (2020)

A mythical fox disguised as a modern man protects people from dangerous supernatural creatures and at the same time searches for the reincarnation of his beloved, who died several centuries ago.
